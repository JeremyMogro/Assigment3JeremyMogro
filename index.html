How to Make Fresh Lemon Juice
Lemon juice is a refreshing and healthy drink, easy to make and perfect for hot days. Hereâ€™s how you can make it at home.
Ingredients
- 4 fresh lemons
- 1 liter of water
- Sugar or sweetener to taste
- Ice (optional)
Step 1: Squeeze the Lemons
Cut the lemons in half and squeeze them into a bowl using a hand or electric juicer. Make sure to extract all the juice from each lemon.
If you prefer to sweeten your lemon juice, you can make a simple syrup. Heat one cup of water with one cup of sugar in a saucepan until the sugar dissolves completely.
In a large pitcher, mix the freshly squeezed lemon juice with the liter of water. Add the syrup to taste and stir well. If desired, add ice to chill the drink.
Step 4: Serve and Enjoy
Serve the lemon juice in glasses with ice and garnish with lemon slices if you like. Enjoy your refreshing drink!
Lemon Juice Variations
Add fresh mint for a herbal twist
Add a touch of ginger for a spicy kick
Use honey instead of sugar for a natural sweetener
You can add soda to make it a sparkling lemonade
Experiment with these ingredients to create your perfect version of lemon juice!
